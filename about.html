Our Story

Founded on Friendship,
Built on Excellence

Nubelle is a fashion retail brand that specialises in luxury-inspired yet affordable bags and sandals for both men and women. This company was built by Silmi with the help of his friends that design and consult the quality which is Syamimi and Alisa as well as with the help of Zam as the head of marketing and Irfan, the head of Production in making sure the business is thriving.

The brand “Nubelle” focuses on simplicity, elegance and practicality by making sure the products being created and released are suitable for daily wear, work environments and casual or special occasions. We believe Nubelle positions in the fashion industry as a brand that bridges the gap between high-end fashion and affordability.
