Shipping Policy

Bringing Nubelle directly to your doorstep.

1. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-3 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.

2. Domestic Shipping (Malaysia)

We are proud to be based in Kuala Lumpur. For our local customers:

  • Standard Shipping: MYR 8.00 (West Malaysia) / MYR 15.00 (East Malaysia).
  • Free Shipping: Available for all orders over MYR 200.
  • Estimated Time: 2-5 business days.

3. International Shipping

We ship globally to select countries including Singapore, Indonesia, and Brunei.

  • Rates are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.
  • International orders may be subject to import taxes/duties, which are the responsibility of the customer.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number to monitor the journey of your package.
