Preserving the Legacy

A Guide to Caring for Your Nubelle Essentials

"Quality improves with care."

Our products are crafted to be companions for your daily journey. To ensure they age beautifully and maintain their structure, we recommend following these simple yet essential care rituals.

For Your Collection

Handbag Care

  • Climate Control Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading and material cracking.
  • Gentle Cleansing Clean gently with a soft, dry, or slightly damp microfiber cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals.
  • Maintain Structure When not in use, keep bags in a dust bag and fill lightly with tissue paper to retain form.
  • Mindful Usage Avoid overloading your bag with heavy items to preserve the integrity of the straps.
Step With Care

Footwear Care

  • Post-Wear Routine Wipe clean after every use to remove surface dust and street dirt.
  • Moisture Management Avoid prolonged contact with water. If exposed, allow sandals to air-dry naturally.
  • Proper Storage Store in a dry, well-ventilated area. Use shoe trees or stuffing for closed-toe styles.
  • Leather Protection For leather sandals, consider applying a specialized conditioner every few months.
Universal Guidelines

The Nubelle Standard

Handle with Care

Treat every clasp, zipper, and stitch with gentleness. Rough handling is the primary cause of premature wear.

Follow Instructions

Always refer to the specific care card provided with your purchase for material-specific nuances.

Long-Term Value

Proper maintenance not only extends the lifespan of your item but preserves its value as a vintage piece.
